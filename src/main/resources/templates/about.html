About Smart Contact Manager

Organize your connections with ease and security

Smart Contact Manager is a modern, secure platform designed to help you manage your contacts efficiently. Whether you're a professional managing business contacts or someone who wants to keep in touch with friends and family, our tool provides everything you need.

Key Features

🔐

Secure Authentication

Login with Google, GitHub, or email/password – your data stays safe.

📇

Unlimited Contacts

Store as many contacts as you need with rich details and profile pictures.

🔍

Advanced Search

Find contacts quickly by name, email, or phone number.

🏷️

Smart Tagging

Organize contacts with custom tags for easy grouping.

📧

Email Reminders

Set reminders and never miss an important follow-up.

☁️

Cloud Storage

Profile pictures stored securely in the cloud.

Our Mission

We empower you to build and maintain meaningful relationships by providing a reliable, intuitive, and secure contact management solution that works everywhere.

Built with Spring Boot 3, Java 21, Thymeleaf, and Tailwind CSS.
